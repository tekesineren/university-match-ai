Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

University Match AI ("We", "Our", "Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

  • Academic Information: GPA, language test scores (TOEFL/IELTS)
  • Educational Background: Background information and field of study
  • Motivation Letters: Text content of your motivation letters
  • CV/Resume: Documents you upload for analysis

2.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide university matching services
  • Improve our application
  • Enhance user experience
  • Fulfill legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • With service providers (hosting, analytics) who have access to data

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure servers
  • Regular security updates

6. Cookies

Our application may use cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use our service. If you delete your account, your data will be deleted within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our application is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes.
